Our Story

About Rose SLI

Bridging communication between the Deaf and hearing communities in San Diego with professionalism and cultural expertise.

Mission

Why We Exist

Rose Sign Language Interpreting was founded on a simple belief: every person deserves access to clear, accurate communication — regardless of hearing ability. We partner with organizations across San Diego and beyond to make that happen, one assignment at a time.

Our interpreters are nationally certified (NIC/RID), culturally fluent, and trained to maintain the highest standards of confidentiality and professionalism in every setting.

ADA & Legal Compliance

Providing qualified interpreters is not just good practice — it is often a legal requirement under the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA), Section 504 of the Rehabilitation Act, and other federal and state statutes. Rose SLI helps your organization meet those obligations confidently.
