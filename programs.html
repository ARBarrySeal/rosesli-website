Specialized interpretation programs designed for the unique demands of each industry and setting in San Diego — and via VRI nationwide.

Corporate & Business

From board meetings to all-hands events, Rose SLI provides experienced interpreters who understand corporate culture and vocabulary. We help your organization communicate inclusion at every level.

Conferences & Events

Program 02

Medical & Healthcare

View dedicated Medical page →

Healthcare communication is life-critical. Our medical interpreters are trained in clinical terminology, HIPAA compliance, and the emotional sensitivity required in patient-facing environments.

Clinical Appointments

Primary care, specialist visits, pre-op, and post-op consultations.

Mental Health

Therapy, psychiatric evaluations, and crisis interventions.

Hospital Settings

Emergency departments, inpatient care, and surgical support.

Telehealth

Remote video interpreting for virtual care platforms.

Program 03

Legal & Government

View dedicated Legal page →

Legal proceedings demand absolute accuracy. Rose SLI interpreters are experienced in courtroom protocol, legal terminology, and the ethical obligations of legal interpreting.

Courtroom

Trials, hearings, arraignments, and sentencing proceedings.

Depositions

Certified interpreting for legal depositions and sworn testimony.

Government Services

DMV, social services, public hearings, and government agency appointments.

ADA Compliance

Helping municipalities and agencies meet federal accessibility mandates.
