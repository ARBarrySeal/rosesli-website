Founder & Director

AR

Amanda Rose

Founder & Lead Interpreter

Amanda founded Rose SLI after more than a decade of interpreting in medical, legal, and educational settings across San Diego. Nationally certified (NIC) and a graduate of the Palomar College Interpreter Education Program, she built Rose SLI on the principle that every Deaf individual deserves a qualified interpreter — not just an available one.

Amanda is an active member of the Registry of Interpreters for the Deaf (RID) and the California Coalition of Agencies Serving the Deaf and Hard of Hearing (CCASDHH).

Credentials

Our Certifications

Every Rose SLI interpreter holds active national certification and participates in ongoing continuing education to maintain the highest standards of practice.

NIC — National Interpreter Certification

The national standard for ASL interpreting, issued by the Registry of Interpreters for the Deaf (RID).

RID Membership

Active members of the national professional association with adherence to the RID Code of Professional Conduct.

Specialty Training

Additional certifications in medical, legal, and educational interpreting for specialized assignments.

CEU Compliance

All interpreters maintain continuing education unit requirements through approved RID/ACET providers.
