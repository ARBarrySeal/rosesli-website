What We Do

Our Services

Corporate & Business

From board meetings to company-wide all-hands events, our interpreters bring corporate vocabulary fluency and discretion to every engagement. We support staff meetings, HR sessions, onboarding, training, and executive conferences — on-site or via VRI.

Medical & Healthcare

Healthcare communication is life-critical. Our HIPAA-trained interpreters are fluent in clinical terminology and deliver accurate, compassionate interpretation for appointments, procedures, mental health sessions, and emergency settings across San Diego County.

Legal & Government

Legal proceedings demand absolute accuracy and strict confidentiality. Rose SLI interpreters are experienced in courtroom protocol, legal terminology, and ethical obligations — from depositions and trials to ADA compliance hearings and public meetings.

Education

From elementary classrooms to university lecture halls, we provide consistent, qualified interpreters who support Deaf and hard-of-hearing students and faculty. We cover IEP meetings, classroom instruction, professional development, and virtual learning.

Nationwide VRI Available — Our Video Remote Interpreting service connects you with a certified ASL interpreter anywhere in the U.S., within minutes. No travel required.
