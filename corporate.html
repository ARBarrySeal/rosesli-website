Why It Matters

ADA Compliance Starts with Communication

Under Title I and Title III of the Americans with Disabilities Act, employers and places of public accommodation are required to provide effective communication access for employees and customers who are Deaf or hard-of-hearing. Providing a qualified ASL interpreter is one of the most direct ways to meet this obligation — and to demonstrate a genuine commitment to inclusion.

Rose SLI makes ADA compliance straightforward. Our certified interpreters are experienced in corporate environments, familiar with business vocabulary, and understand the importance of discretion and professionalism in sensitive workplace settings.

Remote Interpreting

Nationwide VRI for Corporate Teams

Your Deaf employees and clients shouldn't be limited by geography. Rose SLI's Video Remote Interpreting (VRI) service connects you with a certified ASL interpreter via secure video — anywhere in the United States, within minutes.

How VRI Works

An interpreter joins your virtual meeting or appears on a dedicated device at your location. They interpret in real time, exactly as an on-site interpreter would — with none of the travel time or coordination overhead.

When VRI is Ideal

Distributed teams, last-minute meetings, locations outside San Diego, telehealth visits, or any situation where you need a qualified interpreter faster than travel allows. VRI is also a cost-effective solution for shorter engagements.

VRI Platforms We Support

We work with Zoom, Microsoft Teams, Google Meet, WebEx, Skype, and other major video conferencing platforms. We can also deploy VRI on a tablet or laptop at your physical location.
